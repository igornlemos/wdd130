Our Rafting Trips

Choose Your Adventure

Whether you're a first-timer looking for a gentle float or an experienced rafter seeking extreme rapids, we have the perfect trip for you. All trips include professional guides, safety equipment, and unforgettable memories!

Featured Adventures

Family Fun Float

Duration: Half Day (4 hours)

Difficulty: Class I-II Rapids

Price: $75 per person

Perfect for families and beginners! This gentle trip down the Lower Canyon features calm waters, small rapids, and plenty of opportunities to swim and enjoy the scenery. Children as young as 5 are welcome with adult supervision. Our guides will teach you basic paddling techniques and river safety while you relax and soak in the beautiful surroundings.

Canyon Explorer

Duration: Full Day (8 hours)

Difficulty: Class III Rapids

Price: $150 per person

Our most popular trip! Navigate through the stunning Middle Canyon with moderate rapids that provide excitement without being overwhelming. This full-day adventure includes a riverside lunch, opportunities for cliff jumping, and breathtaking views of towering canyon walls. Minimum age is 12 years old. You'll experience the thrill of whitewater while still having time to appreciate the natural beauty around you.

Extreme Rapids Challenge

Duration: Full Day (8 hours)

Difficulty: Class IV-V Rapids

Price: $225 per person

For experienced rafters only! Take on the intense Upper Canyon featuring massive waves, steep drops, and heart-pounding rapids. This adrenaline-pumping adventure requires previous rafting experience and excellent physical fitness. Participants must be 18 or older and pass a swim test. Your expert guide will lead you through challenging rapids with names like "The Widowmaker" and "Devil's Drop." This is the ultimate test of skill and courage!
